# Campaign-Performance-project
Campaign Performance Analysis is an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes customer interactions, campaign types, marketing channels, conversions, website engagement, and sales. It provides actionable insights to evaluate campaign performance, optimize marketing strategies, improve customer engagement, and support data driven decisions.

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to evaluate the performance of marketing campaigns. By analyzing customer interactions, campaign types, marketing channels, customer segments, website engagement, conversions, and sales, the dashboard provides valuable insights into campaign effectiveness and supports data-driven marketing decisions.

## Business Objective

The objective of this analysis is to identify the marketing strategies that generate the highest customer engagement, conversion rates, and sales. These insights help businesses optimize marketing spend, improve campaign performance, and maximize return on investment (ROI).

## Key Business Questions

* Which campaign type generates the highest sales?
* Which marketing channel delivers the best conversion rate?
* Which customer segment contributes the most revenue?
* Does time spent on the website influence customer conversions?
* Which campaigns provide the strongest overall business performance?

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

* Executive KPI Cards
* Sales by Campaign Type
* Conversion Rate by Channel
* Sales by Customer Type
* Time on Site vs. Sales Analysis
* Campaign Performance Treemap
* Conversion Status Breakdown
* Customer Journey Funnel
* Interactive Filters for Campaign Type, Channel, and Customer Type

## Key Insights

The analysis revealed that campaign performance varies across marketing channels and customer segments. Email campaigns generated the strongest sales and highest conversion rates, while customers who spent more time on the website were more likely to complete a purchase. Returning customers also contributed a significant share of total revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention strategies. These findings demonstrate that optimizing campaign targeting and customer engagement can significantly improve marketing performance.

## Recommendations

* Increase investment in high-performing marketing channels.
* Improve website engagement to boost conversion rates.
* Develop personalized campaigns for different customer segments.
* Focus on customer retention through targeted loyalty initiatives.
* Continuously monitor campaign performance using interactive dashboards.

## Conclusion

This Power BI dashboard transforms marketing data into actionable business insights, enabling stakeholders to evaluate campaign effectiveness, optimize marketing strategies, and make informed decisions. The project demonstrates practical skills in Power BI, DAX, data visualization, and business intelligence while showcasing how analytics can support better business outcomes.
